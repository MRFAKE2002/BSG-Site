Here are some highlights of our telecommunication solutions at sea:

Satellite Communication

We provide state-of-the-art satellite communication systems that enable vessels to establish robust and high-quality connections, even in remote and offshore areas.
Our solutions utilize advanced satellite technology to deliver reliable voice and data communication services.

Broadband Internet Connectivity

Voice Communication

We understand the importance of clear and uninterrupted voice communication on board.
Our telecommunication solutions include voice services that ensure reliable and secure communication among crew members, as well as with onshore teams and emergency services.

Crew Welfare Services

We prioritize the well-being of crew members on board.
Our telecommunication solutions encompass crew welfare services, such as internet access, VoIP calling, and entertainment platforms, enabling crew members to stay connected with their loved ones, access online resources, and enjoy onboard entertainment during their time at sea.
